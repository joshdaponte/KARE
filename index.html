Susan Kare

About The Artist

Susan Kare is an influential artist and designer who, since 1983, has created thousands of software icons that communicate function immediately and memorably.

In 1982, she was approached by Andy Hertzfeld to sketch icons for Apple's Macintosh computer. Computers were making the transition into homes, and they needed to be accessible, requiring a visual language the average person could use. Although she lacked technical training, and was confined to a 32x32 canvas, she was able to develop icons that had personality and were approachable.
